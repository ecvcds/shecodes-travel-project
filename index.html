Why Los Angeles?

The City of Angels

Los Angeles is a vibrant and iconic metropolis located in Southern California, USA. Renowned for its glitz, glamour, and entertainment industry, the city holds a magnetic appeal that draws people from all walks of life. Boasting a stunning coastline, it offers a perfect blend of urban sprawl and natural beauty. Los Angeles is a melting pot of diverse cultures, evident in its diverse neighborhoods and delectable culinary scene. As the heart of the global film and television industry, Hollywood stands as a symbol of the city's artistic creativity and cinematic history. Beyond the fame and fortune, Los Angeles also embraces a thriving arts scene, world-class museums, and captivating landmarks like the Griffith Observatory and the iconic Hollywood Sign. Embracing the spirit of ambition and innovation, Los Angeles continues to evolve, making it a dynamic and exciting destination for residents and visitors alike.

My favourite cafés in Los Angeles

Crossroads Kitchen

Located in West Hollywood, Crossroads Kitchen is a trendy and upscale vegan restaurant that elevates plant-based dining to new heights. Helmed by renowned vegan chef Tal Ronnen, the restaurant offers a diverse menu of gourmet dishes crafted with seasonal, locally sourced ingredients.

Address

8284 Melrose Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90046, United States

What is different about it?

From their famous artichoke "oysters" and truffle macaroni to mouthwatering desserts, Crossroads Kitchen provides an unforgettable dining experience for vegans and non-vegans alike.

Shojin Organic & Natural

For those seeking a taste of vegan Japanese cuisine, Shojin is a must-visit. With locations in downtown Los Angeles and Culver City, Shojin offers an authentic and creative vegan take on traditional Japanese dishes.

Address

12406 Washington Blvd, Los Angeles CA 90066, United States

What is different about it?

rom sushi rolls and sashimi to tempura and ramen, their menu caters to a diverse range of palates. All their dishes are thoughtfully prepared with organic and natural ingredients, providing a truly unique and satisfying dining experience for vegans and sushi lovers alike.

Gracias Madre

Situated in the heart of West Hollywood, Gracias Madre is a plant-based Mexican restaurant known for its vibrant atmosphere and delectable dishes.

Address

8905 Melrose Ave, West Hollywood, CA 90069, United States

What is different about it?

Embracing traditional Mexican flavors, the restaurant serves up a variety of vegan tacos, enchiladas, and quesadillas, all made with organic, locally sourced ingredients. Their inventive cocktail menu and a charming outdoor patio add to the overall experience, making it a popular choice for vegans and Mexican food enthusiasts.
